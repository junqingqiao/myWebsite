MY ROBOT

Leader: Junqing Qiao (me)

members: Kuan Yang (my good friend), ZhangQiao Li, Hongbin Zhu (my roommate), Xuesong Chen

        During the summer vacation of 2009( 6/2009~10/2009), I lead a five people team designed and made a transformable human-like robot and a car that can go along lines drew on the road automaticly to assist the robot. I was the team leader and programmer of this small team. We made this robot to attend the robot wrestling competition.

         There are creatively technologys in our robot. Such as:
         Shaking is always a common defect for robot using steering engines as dymamic system. After some test, I think the shaking is caused by the over-move of the steering engines due to the large inertance.So I came up with the "inertance resistance function". The inertance resistance fuction can control the torsion on the steering engines precisely to avoid over-move with the information of the mess of objects linked to engines. By putting the inertance resistance function in my steering engine control program I solved this problem successfully.       
         The remote controler is also a small creation. I modified a computer game joystick’s inner circuits. And connect the joystick to our self-made wireless device. A cool meeting the ergonomics design robot-wireless-control appeared!

         The problems I faced:
         The most difficult problems I faced during this project are not some technical problems. They are how to make the team’s efficiency higher, how to make the right decision as a team leader.
         Some of my teammates’ enthusiasms are drained away by the difficult work. The efficiency of my team is lower and lower. A good job plan may be a solution. But it’s only a temporary one.
There are always some debates in my team. It seems that some people will never accept the other’s opinion. Much of time is wasted on debating.
         Trying to solve those problems, I learned a lot about leadership. I think the best way to improve efficiency on research works is inspire the others, making them pride of what they have done. Facing debates democracy may work, but at some time dictatorship is also needed.
